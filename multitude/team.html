The Team

Appolysus was started with a vision to bring in a revolution and create a deep-seated impact in the lives of men and women. Just like a person or an establishment is remembered for their work, it is envisaged that this endeavour shall set ourselves in firm footing on the corridors of collective human memory. We believe that in our effort to change the lives of people, we are simultaneously opening up avenues that would endow bright minds with the liberty of free thought, the seeds to any major historical change in this world. Therefore, we prefer to regard this endeavour of ours, as a revolution. Just like any other that shook the world. Hence with the tagline "Creating Geniuses Revolutionizing Mankind", Appolysus was born on December, 2013

In the current picture we believe we can inspire all the young minds like us who want to build something of their own. Who want to leave a mark which will remain indelible and be celebrated forever? We would proudly say take a look at us. We might not be the best programmers, the best managers, and the best of each detail a company defines. But we are the best because when you find us awake late night with our laptops after a long day at college/ office, after meeting all the academic, family and social problems, responsibilities of everyday life… We share a common dream, a motivation, a zeal, a dream or a vision whatever you may define it as "We will create history...” That love keeps us going. That believes of achievement binds us together
